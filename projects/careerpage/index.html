How do you intrigue passionate people to work for your company?

About 8th Light

8th Light is a software consultancy that is passionate about crafting beautiful and durable software products. They work through an agile, iterative process that emphasizes clean, deployable code, and maintains transparency with their clients every step along the way. They foster productive partnerships with clients to help discover and solve their most challenging problems, and deliver software solutions that are durable, extensible, and prepared to handle even those future demands that nobody could anticipate.

01. Project Overview

Since the dawn of 8th Light's existence it has hired exclusively through its apprenticeship program. For that reason, when a curious candidate would come to 8thlight.com they would find a page dedicated to the apprenticeship program and its different levels. What we were finding was that candidates were still constantly emailing questions regarding the apprenticeship program, what position was right for them, and what a career at 8th Light looks like.

02. Group Exercise

Being a designer at 8th Light I knew what I thought a "career at 8th Light" meant but I wanted to make sure we took some time as a group to really dive in and create a comprehensive list of what "A Career at 8th Light" meant to us.

03. Interviews

The next step I took was to conduct interviews with people of a variety of backgrounds and interests about their experience applying to 8th Light and what they look for when searching for a job position.

A few findings:

  • Before applying to 8th Light: Our applicants range from considering themselves a “Career Switcher”, beginner, mid-level engineer, or senior level developer
  • Our applicants are looking for a career where they can learn or grow
  • Our applicants want to know they will be working on interesting projects
  • They want someplace that has a good culture
  • They are interested in a place that has core values and practices
  • They appreciate a company that values their background, and how their unique skills help them fit in
  • How is 8th Light different from any of their other experiences? "People actually care about their job and doing it well"
  • How is 8th Light different from any of their other experiences? "Direct interaction with your clients helps you understand the problem better"

04. Design Process

In creating the new career page I wanted to keep a few things in mind:

  • Candidates should be given information about their role within 8th Light so they can see how they might fit into the big picture
  • Candidates should be given information about their team and who they would be working with
  • Candidates should be given information on what it means to be an apprentice and mentor
  • We want to show not just tell our candidates about our culture
  • We should be transparent about the benefits that 8th Light offers.
  • We should emphasize different ways that 8th Light promotes continual learning
  • We want to make sure that candidates are staying on our site and conversions are high around applying and reading about our apprenticeship program
