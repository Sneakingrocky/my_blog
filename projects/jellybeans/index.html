How do you help people track and accomplish their goals?

About Jellybeans

Jellybeans was a side project to design a platform for tracking your career goals. Users stay motivated by tracking their progress, as well as seeing the accomplishments of their teammates and peers.

My Role: Lead Designer

01. Signup Process

02. Profile Page Features

  • Ability to Add a new goal easily in from the top navigation bar
  • Ability to see their performance overtime for total goals made, completed, completion rate, and longest streak
  • Ability to view which categories their goals are so they can see where they need to adjust their focus
  • Ability to view their current goals with their progress and checklist that breaks down the goal into smaller actionable pieces
  • Ability to make private goals that they do not wish to share with others
  • Ability to track the progress of their peers/mentees so they know when a teammate might need a little motivation

03. Team page Features

Teams and clubs can create a profile to track their goals and progress

04. Activity Screen

From the activity screen a user can view recent goals made by other teammates or teams. They are also able to search for any member or team profile to see their public goals and progress.
