About Garcia Design Jewelry

In 2013, I had an itch to create a product with my hands and sell it. I wasn't really sure what that would be and experimented with a variety of different mediums such as intricate paper cutting designs, watercolor paintings, and charcoal drawings. I remember talking to the most wonderful person in the world - my mother. She was admiring my drawings and said "You used to make a lot of jewelry growing up. How about you combine your drawings with jewelry making?". This idea would lead to me selling my work in galleries, and all over the world.
